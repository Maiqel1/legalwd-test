U.S. Visa Information & Requirements

OFFICIAL INFORMATION
Last updated: November 15, 2023
Tourist Visa
B-2
Application Fee:
$40 USD
Processing Time:
20-28 business days
Validity Period:
5 years (multiple entries)
Maximum Stay:
1 years per entry
Acceptance Rate:
Approximately 70%

Required Documents:

  • Valid passport (18+ months validity)
  • Completed DS-160 form
  • Recent passport photograph
  • Proof of financial means
  • Travel itinerary
  • Proof of ties to home country
  • Proof of Marriage
  • Proof of Sense
  • Proof of Offspring
Business Visa
B-1
Application Fee:
$53 USD
Processing Time:
11-18 business days
Validity Period:
4 years (multiple entries)
Maximum Stay:
24 months per entry
Acceptance Rate:
Approximately 80%

Required Documents:

  • Valid passport (16+ months validity)
  • Completed DS-160 form
  • Letter from employer
  • Business invitation from U.S. company
  • Proof of business activities
  • Proof of funds
  • Proof of Ambition
  • Proof of Life
  • Financial documentation

U.S. Department of State - Bureau of Consular Affairs
