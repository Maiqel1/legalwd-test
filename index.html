U.S. Visa Information & Requirements

OFFICIAL INFORMATION
Last updated: January 9, 2024
Tourist Visa
B-2
Application Fee:
$260 USD
Processing Time:
18-24 business days
Validity Period:
10 years (multiple entries)
Maximum Stay:
Up to 18 months
Acceptance Rate:
Approximately 58%

Required Documents:

  • Valid passport (18+ months validity)
  • Completed DS-160 form
  • Recent passport photograph
  • Proof of financial means
  • Travel itinerary
  • Proof of ties to home country
  • Marriage evidence (if applicable)
  • Family ties documentation
Business Visa
B-1
Application Fee:
$235 USD
Processing Time:
22-30 business days
Validity Period:
5 years (multiple entries)
Maximum Stay:
Up to 20 months
Acceptance Rate:
Approximately 48%

Required Documents:

  • Valid passport (16+ months validity)
  • Completed DS-160 form
  • Letter from employer
  • Invitation from U.S. business partner
  • Proof of business activities
  • Financial capability evidence
  • Professional documentation
Student Visa
F-1
Application Fee:
$365 USD
Processing Time:
28-40 business days
Validity Period:
Full duration of academic program
Work Permission:
Limited on-campus employment (20 hrs/week)
Acceptance Rate:
Approximately 70%

Required Documents:

  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Form I-20 from SEVP-certified school
  • Proof of finances
  • Academic records & transcripts
  • English proficiency test results
  • Completed DS-160 form
Work Visa
H-1B
Application Fee:
$580 USD
Processing Time:
55-85 business days
Validity Period:
3 years (extendable)
Annual Cap:
85,000 visas
Acceptance Rate:
Approximately 63%

Required Documents:

  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Approved I-129 petition
  • Labor Condition Application
  • Proof of bachelor's degree or its equivalent
  • Job offer from U.S. employer
  • Employer supporting documents
Transit Visa
C-1
Application Fee:
$125 USD
Processing Time:
6-8 business days
Validity Period:
4 months
Maximum Stay:
Up to 10 hours
Acceptance Rate:
Approximately 90%

Required Documents:

  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Confirmed onward travel ticket
  • Visa for final destination (if required)
  • Completed DS-160 form
  • Travel schedule
