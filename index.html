Student Visa
F-1
Application Fee:
$160 USD
Processing Time:
25-35 business days
Validity Period:
Duration of program + 60 days
Maximum Stay:
Depends on program length
Approval Rate:
Approximately 85%

Required Documents:

  • Valid passport (6+ months beyond stay)
  • Completed DS-160 form
  • I-20 form from U.S. school
  • SEVIS fee receipt
  • Proof of financial ability
  • Academic transcripts
  • Standardized test scores (if required)
  • Visa interview confirmation
Exchange Visitor Visa
J-1
Application Fee:
$220 USD
Processing Time:
20-30 business days
Validity Period:
Depends on exchange program
Maximum Stay:
12-36 months
Approval Rate:
Approximately 80%

Required Documents:

  • Valid passport (6+ months beyond stay)
  • Completed DS-160 form
  • DS-2019 form from program sponsor
  • Proof of financial support
  • Medical insurance coverage
  • Letter from sponsor or organization
  • Visa interview appointment confirmation
  • Proof of intent to return home
