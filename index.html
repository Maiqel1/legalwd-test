Academic Student Visa
F-1
Application Fee:
$205 USD
Processing Time:
10-90 calendar days
Validity Period:
Up to 5 years (multiple entry)
Maximum Stay:
Duration of Status (D/S) + 60 day grace period
SEVIS Fee:
$350 (additional)

Required Documents:

  • Passport valid for at least 6 months beyond intended stay
  • Form DS-160 confirmation page with barcode
  • Form I-20 signed by school official
  • SEVIS I-901 fee payment receipt
  • Financial documents covering first year of study
  • Academic transcripts and diplomas
  • Standardized test scores (if required by program)
  • Digital photograph meeting specifications
  • Visa interview appointment confirmation
  • Proof of intent to return home after studies
Cultural Exchange Visa
J-1
Application Fee:
$205 USD
Processing Time:
15-120 calendar days
Validity Period:
Program duration + 30 days grace period
Maximum Stay:
Varies by category (1-5 years typically)
SEVIS Fee:
$220 (additional)

Required Documents:

  • Passport with at least 6 months validity
  • Form DS-160 barcode confirmation
  • Form DS-2019 (Certificate of Eligibility)
  • Proof of SEVIS fee payment
  • Program sponsor documents
  • Proof of medical insurance
  • Evidence of ties to home country
  • Proof of English language ability
  • Two-color passport photos
  • Resume/CV for certain categories
Business Visitor Visa
B-1
Application Fee:
$205 USD
Processing Time:
7-120 business days (varies by embassy)
Validity Period:
Typically 1-10 years (multiple entry)
Maximum Stay:
Up to 6 months per entry (CBP discretion)
Interview Waiver:
Available for renewals under certain conditions

Required Documents:

  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Completed Form DS-160 with confirmation
  • Letter from U.S. company/organization
  • Letter from home country employer
  • Proof of sufficient funds for trip
  • Evidence of strong ties to home country
  • Detailed business itinerary
  • Proof of previous international travel
  • Business registration documents (if self-employed)
  • Conference registration (if attending event)
