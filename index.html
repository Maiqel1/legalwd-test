Academic Student Visa
F-9
Application Fee:
$160 USD
Processing Time:
10-30 calendar days
Validity Period:
Up to 5 years (multiple entry)
Maximum Stay:
D/S + 60-day grace period
SEVIS Fee:
$305 (additional)

Required Documents:

  • Passport valid 6+ months beyond stay
  • DS-160 confirmation page
  • Form I-20 signed by school
  • SEVIS fee payment receipt
  • Bank statements for first year
  • Academic transcripts and diplomas
  • Test scores if required
  • Digital photo meeting requirements
  • Visa interview confirmation
  • Proof of intent to return home
Cultural Exchange Visa
J-1
Application Fee:
$245 USD
Processing Time:
20-50 calendar days
Validity Period:
Program duration + 45-day grace period
Maximum Stay:
1-5 years depending on category
SEVIS Fee:
$25 (additional)

Required Documents:

  • Passport with 6+ months validity
  • DS-160 confirmation page
  • Form DS-2019
  • SEVIS fee receipt
  • Sponsor program documents
  • Medical insurance proof
  • Evidence of home country ties
  • English proficiency proof
  • Two passport photos
  • Resume/CV if applicable
Business Visitor Visa
B-1
Application Fee:
$325 USD
Processing Time:
15-60 business days
Validity Period:
5-10 years (multiple entry)
Maximum Stay:
Up to 6 months per visit
Interview Waiver:
Available for certain renewals

Required Documents:

  • Valid passport (6+ months)
  • Completed DS-160 form
  • Letter from U.S. organization
  • Letter from home employer
  • Proof of sufficient funds
  • Evidence of strong ties to home country
  • Business itinerary
  • Previous travel history
  • Business registration (if self-employed)
  • Conference/event registration if applicable
