Visitor Visa
B-2
Application Fee:
$90 USD
Processing Time:
15-25 business days
Validity Period:
3 years (multiple entries)
Maximum Stay:
6 months per entry
Approval Rate:
Approximately 72%

Required Documents:

  • Passport valid for at least 12 months
  • Completed DS-160 form
  • Recent passport-size photo
  • Bank statements or proof of funds
  • Confirmed travel itinerary
  • Employment or education proof
  • Marriage certificate (if applicable)
  • Birth certificate (if traveling with children)
Professional Visa
B-1
Application Fee:
$75 USD
Processing Time:
10-20 business days
Validity Period:
5 years (multiple entries)
Maximum Stay:
12 months per entry
Approval Rate:
Approximately 78%

Required Documents:

  • Valid passport with at least 12 months validity
  • Completed DS-160 form
  • Employer letter explaining purpose of visit
  • Invitation letter from U.S. company
  • Proof of ongoing business activities
  • Bank statements showing sufficient funds
  • Professional credentials or certificates
  • Resume or CV

U.S. Department of State - Consular Affairs Division
