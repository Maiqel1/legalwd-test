U.S. Visa Information & Requirements

OFFICIAL INFORMATION
Last updated: November 15, 2023
Tourist Visa
B-2
Application Fee:
$160 USD
Processing Time:
10-15 business days
Validity Period:
10 years (multiple entries)
Maximum Stay:
6 months per entry
Acceptance Rate:
Approximately 85%

Required Documents:

  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Completed DS-160 form
  • Recent passport photograph
  • Proof of financial means
  • Travel itinerary
  • Proof of ties to home country
Business Visa
B-1
Application Fee:
$190 USD
Processing Time:
12-18 business days
Validity Period:
1 year (multiple entries)
Maximum Stay:
6 months per entry
Acceptance Rate:
Approximately 79%

Required Documents:

  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Completed DS-160 form
  • Letter from employer
  • Business invitation from U.S. company
  • Proof of business activities
  • Financial documentation
Student Visa
F-1
Application Fee:
$350 USD
Processing Time:
30-45 business days
Validity Period:
Duration of study program
Work Permission:
Limited on-campus employment
Acceptance Rate:
Approximately 72%

Required Documents:

  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Form I-20 from SEVP-certified school
  • Proof of financial support
  • Academic transcripts and diplomas
  • English proficiency test results
  • Completed DS-160 form
Work Visa
H-1B
Application Fee:
$555 USD
Processing Time:
60-90 business days
Validity Period:
3 years (extendable to 6)
Annual Cap:
85,000 visas
Acceptance Rate:
Approximately 65%

Required Documents:

  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Form I-129 petition by employer
  • Labor Condition Application (LCA)
  • Proof of bachelor's degree or equivalent
  • Job offer letter
  • Employer support documents
Transit Visa
C-1
Application Fee:
$120 USD
Processing Time:
5-7 business days
Validity Period:
3 months
Maximum Stay:
Up to 12 hours
Acceptance Rate:
Approximately 92%

Required Documents:

  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Confirmed onward ticket
  • Visa for final destination (if required)
  • Completed DS-160 form
  • Travel itinerary
